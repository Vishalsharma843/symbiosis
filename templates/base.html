About us

We are academics and sisterhood collectives' aficionados and work on indie music, popular music, women musicians, Creative Industries and collaborative work in the Creative Industries. We have tried to explore this area of study as it has not been researched widely in the context of India. We look at the independent music scene in India, especially the women musicians who strive to grow through building connections and networking. We study this using the concept of sisterhood feminism, where women empower themselves through helpful collaborations, professionally and personally.
